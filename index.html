Agency of Denk Pharma

Supporting Denk Pharma’s high-quality European medicines across Iraqi Kurdistan and Iraq.

In partnership with Denk Pharma GmbH & Co. KG, Al-Khwarizmi Co. provides marketing, medical promotion, sales support, and regulatory coordination for Denk Pharma’s portfolio in Iraqi Kurdistan and Iraq.

Marketing & sales support

Field-force planning, territory coverage analysis, and structured follow-up with doctors, dentists, and pharmacies to support Denk Pharma’s sales growth.

Scientific promotion

Medical and sales teams trained to provide evidence-based information on Denk Pharma products to doctors, dentists, and pharmacists throughout the region.

About our company

Together, Denk Pharma and Al-Khwarizmi Co. combine international quality standards with local market expertise to support market access and responsible use of safe, effective, and affordable medicines.

Our vision

To be the leading strategic agency for Denk Pharma in Iraqi Kurdistan and Iraq, supporting better health outcomes for patients and communities.

Our mission

To support Denk Pharma’s high-quality pharmaceutical portfolio through scientific marketing, medical promotion, sales support, and close collaboration with healthcare professionals and regulatory bodies.

Our values

Quality, transparency, and long-term partnership guide our daily work—from scientific events and field visits to after-sales support and regulatory follow-up.
